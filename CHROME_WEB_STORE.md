**How It Works:**

1. Click the extension icon in your browser
2. Add your AWS accounts with custom names and account IDs
3. When visiting AWS sign-in, click "Connect" on any saved account
4. The extension automatically fills in the account ID
5. Complete your sign-in flow seamlessly

**Permissions Explained:**

- `activeTab`: To detect when you're on AWS sign-in pages
